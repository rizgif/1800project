What is heat stroke?

Heat stroke is a serious medical condition that can occur when your body overheats. It can cause damage to your brain and other vital organs, and in extreme cases, it can be fatal. To prevent heat stroke, it's important to take some simple steps to keep your body cool and hydrated.
